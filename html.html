Seasonal Specialties

Fresh catches prepared daily with seasonal ingredients and regional flavors. At Sea Tales, we celebrate nature's rhythm with our Seasonal Specialties menu, showcasing the ocean's freshest catches at their peak. Each season brings new flavors spring offers tender scallops and wild halibut, summer highlights sweet prawns and grilled sardines, autumn features rich lobster and mussels, while winter presents hearty seafood stews and roasted cod. Our chefs craft these limited-time dishes using locally sourced ingredients and sustainable seafood, paired with seasonal produce for vibrant, balanced flavors. From delicate citrus-infused ceviche in warmer months to comforting bouillabaisse when temperatures drop, every dish reflects the season's essence. Indulge in these exclusive creations before they disappear because the best flavors are those that nature serves fresh.

Multi-Level Dining Experience

From our vibrant ground floor energy to our serene upper-level sophistication, find your perfect dining atmosphere.

Vibrant Ground Floor

Lively social spaces with an open kitchen concept Immerse yourself in the lively energy of our ground floor, where the heart of Sea Tales comes alive. This bustling space features an open kitchen concept, letting you watch our chefs craft seafood masterpieces while the aroma of sizzling dishes fills the air. High ceilings, nautical decor, and warm lighting create a welcoming coastal vibe perfect for social dining. The centerpiece is our raw bar, showcasing fresh oysters and ceviche, while the cocktail bar serves craft drinks with ocean-inspired twists. Large communal tables encourage conversation, making it ideal for groups and casual meetups. Live acoustic music on weekends adds to the vibrant atmosphere. Whether you're here for happy hour or a festive dinner, our ground floor delivers a dynamic seafood experience where every visit feels like a coastal celebration.

Cozy Private Corners

Intimate spaces for special occasions Escape to our intimate private dining nooks, designed for romantic evenings and special celebrations. These secluded spaces feature plush banquettes, soft candlelight, and elegant nautical touches that create a warm, inviting ambiance. Each corner offers discreet privacy while maintaining a connection to our restaurant's vibrant energy. Perfect for anniversaries or proposals, our staff provides attentive yet unobtrusive service. The menu shines with shareable seafood towers and premium wine pairings curated by our sommelier. Sound-absorbing designs ensure quiet conversation flows effortlessly. For business dinners, retractable screens offer meeting privacy without sacrificing our signature coastal charm. Whether celebrating love or closing deals, these hidden gems deliver an exclusive experience within our bustling seafood haven.

Elegant Elevated Dining

Panoramic views with sophisticated ambiance Perched above the bustling main floor, our elevated dining area offers refined sophistication with breathtaking panoramic views. This exclusive space features crisp white linens, polished silverware, and floor-to-ceiling windows framing coastal vistas, creating an atmosphere of timeless luxury. Designed for discerning guests, the menu showcases chef's tasting menus with premium seafood selections like Alaskan king crab and Japanese bluefin tuna, paired with sommelier curated wines. Subdued lighting and acoustically treated spaces ensure intimate conversations, while attentive servers deliver seamless, unobtrusive service. Ideal for milestone celebrations or executive dinners, the elevated experience includes a private balcony for sunset cocktails. Here, every detail from champagne trolleys to tableside preparations elevates dining into an art form.
